Kings Corners Rules

🂡 Phase 1 — Placement

Fill the 4×4 grid one card at a time from a shuffled deck:

  • Kings (K) → corner cells only
  • Queens (Q) → left & right side cells
  • Jacks (J) → top & bottom side cells
  • Ace – 10 → any empty cell

If a face card appears but its zone is full, the game is lost.

🂡 Phase 2 — Elimination

Once the grid is full, remove number cards:

  • Tap any 10 to remove it instantly
  • Tap two cards that sum to 10 (A+9, 2+8, 3+7, 4+6, 5+5)
  • Face cards (J, Q, K) cannot be removed

🂡 Refill

After eliminating, click the deck to deal new cards into the empty slots. Repeat until the deck runs out.

⭐ Scoring

Each card scores its face value when placed or eliminated. Face cards score 10. Higher is better!

💡 Easy Mode

Highlights valid placement cells and eliminable cards. Enables Undo. Score is halved.
